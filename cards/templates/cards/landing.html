Why Spaced Repetition Works

Based on decades of cognitive science research, spaced repetition optimizes when you review to maximize retention.

Defeat the Forgetting Curve

Without review, you forget 70% of new information within 24 hours. Spaced repetition interrupts this decay at the optimal moment.

Study Smarter, Not Harder

Focus your time on cards you're about to forget. Easy cards appear less frequently, hard cards more often.

Build Long-Term Memory

Transform short-term cramming into lasting knowledge. Cards you know well can be scheduled months apart.

How It Works

Three simple steps to supercharge your learning.

1

Create Your Cards

Add flashcards with questions and answers. Use basic cards, cloze deletions, or reversible cards for flexible learning.

2

Review Daily

Spend just a few minutes each day reviewing cards that are due. Rate how well you remembered each one.

3

Watch Your Progress

Track your retention rate, study streaks, and watch cards graduate from "learning" to "mature" status.
